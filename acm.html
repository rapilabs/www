Business agility tools

Adaptive Case Management

Empower your workforce by allowing them to define the process & data requirements with Acme.

Acme is a cloud based product in limited release that utilises the latest HTML5 & database design to achieve a level of flexibility required for SMEs to operate with agility.

Acme incorporates the following features:

  • Process flow can be defined at runtime
  • Processes are defined through process maps and/or tasklists
  • Data storage needs and form based layouts are designed at runtime
  • Templates may be created from scratch or from existing case examples
  • Cases may be spawned from scratch or from a template
